Downbeat Diner & Lounge Website

This local Vegan/Vegetarian all night diner wanted a website as impressive as their menu. Fun, upbeat design that matches the ambiance of the actual restaurant.

  • Responsive Web Design
  • Development

Made to order

When the Downbeat Diner & Lounge asked me to re-design there site, I wanted to make sure it represented their brand. The restaurant is simple, minimalistic and punk rock. So too shall be the website be. It matches the decor and ambiance of the restaurant and offers the user no more than what they need from a restaurant website. Built responsive on the 1140 grid it allows users to check the menu from anywhere.
