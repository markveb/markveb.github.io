Hawaii First Website Development

The community association management firm, Hawaii First, goes above and beyond typical management duties so they needed a website that did the same. We created a total redesign of the site to be more in line with their brand while creating a simple and appealing user experience.

  • Development
  • CMS Integration

Coded by hand, Coded with care

Each line of code was purposefully thought out and written to ensure that the site would be future friendly for browsers and devices. Semantics are the foundation of this sites inner workings and a skillful hand was required to assemble it.

The site was built using the most up to date technologies like HTML5, CSS3. MODX was the content management system of choice due to its extreme flexibility and emphasis on "Creative Freedom".
