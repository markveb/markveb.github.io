Sasquatch Shades Online Store

Sasquatch Shades needed an online store to sell there eco-friendly sunglasses made from natural materials. It needed to match their go green attitude, and laid-back style, letting the shades do the talking.

  • Web Design
  • Development

Beautifully Simple

The simple idea of selling sunglasses made of recycled natural woods is beautiful in its self. It needs an online store that matched that ethos. The site is clean and uncluttered, allowing people to enjoy their shopping experience. Under the hood the site is no different, built on the lightweight CMS Perch and paired with the powerful Foxycart, this site is built for ease.
