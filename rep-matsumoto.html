Hawaii State Rep. Lauren Matsumoto

Hawaii State Representative Lauren Matsumoto needed a new government website to showcase pertinent information to her supporters. Her industry calls for user experience by its very nature. People need to be able to follow her, find information easily, and take action on what they find. A mobile first approach was used also in the development of this project, having done extensive research to confirm the diversity of users, devices, and bandwidth that would be accessing the site.
